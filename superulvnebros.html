CHRISTIAN ULVNE
Composer  ·  Video Game Music  ·  Sweden

Christian Ulvne is a Swedish video game music composer with a sound built from dark cinematic tension, anime-inspired intensity, and the relentless drive of manga action sequences.

Currently scoring the full original soundtrack for Godslayer — a black & white action series that demands music as sharp and heavy as its blade work. His compositions sit at the intersection of orchestral weight and electronic edge.

Available for game projects that need music with weight, darkness, and intent. If your game has a soul, Christian will find its sound.
